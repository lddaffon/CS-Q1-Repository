Ningning

  • Ningning is the stage name of Ning Yi Zhuo, the main vocalist and also the youngest member of aespa. Her birthday is October 23, 2002 and her zodiac sign is Scorpio. Her Korean name is Jeo Ye Tak while her English name is Vivian Ning.
