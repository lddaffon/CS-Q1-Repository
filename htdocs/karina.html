Karina

  • Karina is the official leader and a rapper of aespa under SM Entertainment. Her birth name is Yu Ji-min and her stage name Karina is derived from her baptismal name, Katarina. She was born on April 11, 2000 in South Korea.

  • Source: https://kpop.fandom.com/wiki/Karina_(aespa)

    • ae-KARINA

  • Karina's counterpart in the Kwangya is named ae-KARINA. Together with the virtual counterparts of the other aespa members,ae-KARINA experiences the SYNK-outPhenomenon where the user loses connection with their virtual counterparts. and aespa, with the help of NAVIS, enter Kwangya through their counterparts.
