Winter

  • Winter is the lead vocalist, lead dancer, and official visual of aespa. She was the first member to be revealed by SM Entertainment. Her full name is Kim Min-jeong and she was born on January 1, 2001.

  • Source: https://aespa.fandom.com/wiki/Winter

    • ae-WINTER

  • Winter's counterpart in Kwangya, ae-WINTER, was revealed on November 4, 2020. She has pink hair and brown eyes. In aespa's lore, Winter and ae-WINTER are both ArmamentersComes from the word armament which means military, weapons, or arms..
