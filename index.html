SYNK into aespa


Aespa is a K-pop group by SM Entertainment that debuted on November 17, 2020 with the song "Black Mamba".

They are a four-member girl group consisting of Karina (eldest and official leader), Giselle (main rapper), Winter (official visual), and Ningning (youngest and main vocalist).

Aespa’s fandom name is MY, meaning ‘The Most precious friend’ in Kwangya, the virtual alternate universe created by SM Entertainment and the main setting of aespa's lore.
