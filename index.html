SYNK into aespa

Aespa is a K-pop group by SM Entertainment that debuted on November 17, 2020 with the song "Black Mamba". They are a four-member girl group consisting of Karina (eldest and official leader), Giselle (main rapper), Winter (official visual), and Ningning (youngest and main vocalist).


Aespa’s fandom name is MY, meaning ‘The Most precious friend’ in Kwangya, the virtual alternate universe created by SM Entertainment and the main setting of aespa's lore.

Sources: https://kprofiles.com/aespa-profile/ and https://www.hallyuidol.com/group/x2CIyc8PRXJH9Ahsqgsv


Aespa means "meeting another self and experiencing the new world" on which their concept is based. It is divided into the words "ae" and "spa". The letter combination "ae" are initials for the words "avatar" and "experience", on the other hand, "spa" stands for aspect.

The four members of the aespa lore form a crime-fighting team with their four virtual counterparts, known as aes, in an effort to defeat the villain Black Mamba and save the world.
